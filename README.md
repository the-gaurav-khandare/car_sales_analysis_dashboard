# car_sales_analysis_dashboard

1. Car Sales Analysis

Car Sales Dashboard shows strong performance with $371.2M YTD sales and 13.3K units sold, reflecting solid growth in both revenue and volume.
Average selling price remains stable at $28K, while SUVs and sedans dominate sales, with Pale White & Black as top colors.
Top U.S. cities and brands like Volvo contribute significantly to overall performance.

2. Tech Stack

•	Power BI Dashboard (visualization)
•	Data filters for body style, dealer, transmission, and engine type
•	Interactive charts and map visualization

3. Overview

•	YTD Performance Metrics
    o	Total Sales: $371.2M
    o	Cars Sold: 13.3K units
    o	Avg. Selling Price: $28K
    o	YTD growth visible across revenue and units sold
•	Trend Analysis
    o	Weekly sales trend visualization
    o	Month-to-date vs. year-to-date comparison
•	Sales Breakdown
    o	By Body Style — SUV, Sedan, Hatchback, Passenger, Hardtop
    o	By Color — Pale White, Black, Red, etc.
    o	By Region/Dealer Location (U.S. map view)
•	Brand Performance
    o	Company-wise comparison of:
         • Average price
         •	Cars sold
         •	Total sales
         • % contribution to overall sales
    o	Top performers include: Volvo, Volkswagen, Toyota, Subaru

4. Insights

•	Strong YTD performance with +23.6% revenue growth and +19.7% unit growth
•	Average selling price remains relatively stable
•	SUVs and Sedans lead in sales volume
•	Major sales clusters across key U.S. cities
•	Top automakers show consistent growth and revenue contribution
